## Push Notifications

### Notification Types for Staff:
1. `transaction_confirmed` - Customer confirmed transaction
2. `transaction_rejected` - Customer rejected transaction
3. `payment_received` - Payment received from customer
4. `settlement_ready` - Settlement ready for payout
5. `staff_added` - New staff added to your team
6. `role_updated` - Your role was updated

**Recommended:** Riverpod or BLoC

- `AuthProvider` - Authentication state + role

- `TransactionsProvider` - Transaction list
